Prompt used when calling the Chainlink Confidential AI Attester (POST /v1/inference)
for GoHealthMe health-goal verification.

This is the exact `prompt` field carried in simulation/callback-payload.json and
sent by cre/scripts/call-attester.mjs.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

A GoHealthMe participant committed to a health goal. Based on the attached weekly
health summary, decide whether the goal was met.

Goal: Average >= 8000 steps/day, sustained across the current 7-day window.

Analyze only what is visible in the summary:
- Compute the average daily steps over the 7-day window
- Note the current consecutive on-target streak
- Do not decline due to missing data — assess on available evidence

Respond with ONLY a valid JSON object:
{
  "verified": true,
  "confidence": "high",
  "reason": "one sentence citing specific figures from the summary",
  "metric_value": 8450,
  "threshold": 8000
}
